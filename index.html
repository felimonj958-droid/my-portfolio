Statistical Analysis: Telecom Plan Revenue Optimization
Analyzed 500 Megaline customers across Surf and Ultimate prepaid plans. Applied Welch's t-test to compare average monthly revenue, tested regional differences (NY-NJ vs other areas). Cleaned 137K+ call records, 76K+ messages, 104K+ sessions. Key insights: data aggregation by user-month, billing rule implementation, hypothesis testing with α=0.05. Result: Statistically significant revenue differences identified to inform ad budget allocation.
